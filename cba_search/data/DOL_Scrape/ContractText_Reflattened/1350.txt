PREAMBLE  
This Agreement is entered into by collective bargaining  
to prevent strikes and lockouts and to facilitate peaceful  
adjustment or grievances and disputes between  
Employer and Union in this trade and to prevent waste,  
unnecessary and avoidable delays, and expenses, and,  
so far as possible, to provide for labor's continuous  
employment, such employment to be in accordance with  
the conditions herein set forth and at wages herein agreed  
upon; also, that stable conditions may prevail in the  
building industry and building costs may be as low as  
possible, consistent with fair wages and conditions, and  
further, the establishment of the necessary procedures by  
which these ends may be accomplished.
