Failure to comply within the thirty (30) days will result in the suspension of the employee until certification is obtained and provided to the Company.

ARTICLE 29 - ALCOHOL AND DRUG ABUSE POLICY
For purposes of this Agreement, the terms "drug" or "drug tests" shall include both drugs and alcohol, as appropriate. The Company and the Union recognize that many areasof the Company's operations involve hazardous work with the potential for personal injury or property damage and that al areas involve directly or Indirectly the public at large. Therefore, it must endeavor to provide safe and efficient operations for the protection and benefit of the general public, its customers and its employees. As part of Its effort to achieve that goal, It must require that Its work be performed by employees who do not use illegal drugs or misuse controlled substances and/or alcohol as follows:
SECTION 1.	The Company recognizes that employees have a right to privacy and that any adverse action taken against any employee for off-duty conduct shall take into account the employee's right to privacy and the Impact of foe employee’s conduct on his/her job performance, foe Company's reputation, or foe public's perception of the Company's contract performance. Any disciplinary action for such drug-related conduct will be subject to the grievance procedure.
