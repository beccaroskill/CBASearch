MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING
RE: CONTRACT EXTENSION
This extension agreement is made and entered into by and between the Bay County Library System, doing business at 500 Center located in Bay City, Michigan hereinafter referred to as the "Employer" and the Local 542, AFL-CIO Utility Workers Union of America, hereinafter referred to as "Local 542".
Whereas, the Employer and the Local 542 are parties to a collective bargaining agreement, hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement", covering all full-time employees at the Bay County Library System, but excluding Director, Assistant Directors, Librarians, System Managing Librarians, Administrative Assistant, Financial Analyst, System Coordinators, Marketing Manager, pages, part-time/substitute, temporary and seasonal employees, and employees hired under temporary State or Federal funded programs, effective for the period beginning January 1, 2012 and expiring December 31, 2015.
Whereas, the parties wish to extend the current Agreement for a one year period expiring December 31, 2016, with the attached changes to Section 3, Union Security. The Agreement shall remain in full force until midnight, December 31, 2016 and thereafter as required by Section 12.15 Termination.
