8.01	Should any difference arise between the Com-Danv and the Union or its members employed bv the Company, as to the meaning and direction of the provisions of this Agreement, I such difference shall be taken up and disposed of in the procedures to follow: It is also agreed to by both parties that under no circumstances shall an employee consider that he has a grievance until he has first given his Foreman an opportunity of dealing with the complaint or other matters which may be the source of the grievance.
The employee will be accompanied by his Steward if he desires such assistance. The decision of the Foreman shall be made within two (2) working days.
Step 1
If the Foreman fails to settle the grievance satisfactorily, the Steward shall within five (5) working days of the incident, reduce the grievance to writing on forms to be supplied by the. Union. The Steward shall present the copies of the grievance to the Foreman for nis written disposition, which will be given within two (2) working days.
Step 2
settlement with the Foreman, the aggrieved employee and his Steward or the Chairman of the Union'Executive Committee shall discuss the matter with the .superintendent or his delegated representative within three (3) working, days, and endeavour to effect a settlement. Step 3
All grievances not settled at Step 2 shall be taken up between the Chairman of the Union Executive Committee, an officer of the Union and the Plant Manager and/or designated representatives of the Company within five (5) working days cr the decision given iu Step 2. The Union representative may be included if desired by either party. The decision of the Company shall be given in writing within five (5) working day's following the date of the meeting. If the grievance 'is not settled to the satisfaction ofboth parries, then at the request of either party to the Agreement, the grievance may be referred to Arbitration.

Article 9 ARBITRATION
9.01	When either party requests that a grievance be submitted to Arbitration1, they shall make such request in writing, addressed to the other party to this Agreement, and at the same time, nominate an Arbitrator. Within five (5) working days thereafter, the other party shall nominate an Arbitrator. The. two Arbitrators' so nominated shall meet immediately, and if within two (2) working days they fail to settle the grievance, they shall attempt' to select by agreement, a chairman of an Arbitration Board. If they are unable to agree upon such chairman within a further period of twenty-four (24) hours, they shall then request the Minister of Labour for the Province of Ontario, to assist them in selecting an impartial chairman. If agreement is not reached on an impartial chairman within a further ten (10) days,,, then the Minister of Labour for the Province of Ontario shall be requested to appoint a chairman who shall be a resident of the Province of Ontario.
In the event of default by either party in nominating its representative to the Arbitration Board, the other party may apply to the Minister of Labour for the Pro- 10-
vince of Ontario who shall have power to effect such nominations.
9.02	No person may be appointed as an Arbitrator who has been involved in an attempt to negotiate or settle the grievance.
Bach of the parties hereto will bear the expenses of the Arbitrator appointed by it and the parties will jointly bear the e>menses of the chairman of the Arbitration Board, if any.
No matter may be submitted to Arbitration which has not been properly carried through all previous steps of the Grievance procedure.
9.05	The Arbitration Board shall not be authorized to make any decision inconsistent with the provisions of the Agreement, nor to alter, modify or amend any part of this AgjsemenU.itfir to deal with any matter not covered by this Agreement.
9.06	The proceedings of the Arbitration Board will be expedited by the parties hereto, and the decision of the Chairman or such Board will be final and binding upon the parties hereto.
Any and all time. limits referred to under the Grievance and Arbitration Procedures herein may at any time be extended by written agreement between the Company and the Union or their representatives.
