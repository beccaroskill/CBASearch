PURPOSE
1.01	it is the purpose of this Agreement to maintain and promote harmonious relationship between the Company and its employees, to sei forth hours of work, rates of pay and other conditions of employment and to provide an amicable method of settling any difference or grievance which may arise relating to conditions of employment.
1.02	The parties agree to ensure the betterment of the Leamington Plant (employment levels and year-round operations), a joint Union/Management Committee will be established to review all aspects of the operation.
