It is the intent and purpose of this Agreement that the Company and the Union co-operate to obtain an efficient and unrestricted production and distribution of dairy products, to maintain a harmonious relationship between the Company and its Employees, and to provide a method of settling in an amicable manner any difference or grievance which may from time to time arise, and to set forth rates of pay, hours of work and other conditions of employment to be observed between the parties hereto.

ARTICLE NO. 7 SENIORITY
Seniority shall be based on the length of continuous service an Employee has been on the payroll.

After having completed this probationary period, or upon being recalled within six (6) months after being laid off, the Employee's seniority and service records shall date back to the beginning of his employment.
